  • Describe your ideal work environment.

    I am a laid back intensity junkie. I bounce back and forth from extreme focus to chill and playful. This is my sweetspot.

    I've been told that Einstein would work on intense theories of physics and relativity and when faced with a massive challenge, instead of forcing the answer when he was stuck, he would simply go take a break, and go sailing.
